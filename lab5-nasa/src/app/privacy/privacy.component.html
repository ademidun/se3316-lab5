DMCA TakeDown Procedure
  1. In the collection detail page, accessed via the collections list page. Enter the reason for your complaint and click the file complaint button.
  2. Users can dispute take-down complaints in the edit collection page via my collections. Enter the reason for your dispute and click the File Dispute button.
  3. In the admin page. You can review the complaints and disputes for each item, you have the option to click Remove Takedown Block to re-grant access to the content.
